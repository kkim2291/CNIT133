a) Product number (1 through 5)
b) Quantity sold for that product - (for the week)

Your script should use a switch statement to help determine the retail price for each product and should calculate and display the retail amount for each product and the total retail amount of all the products sold last week. Use form input boxes to obtain the product numbers (hard coded as 1-5) and the quantity sold for each product from the user.
